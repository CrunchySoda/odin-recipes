Mashed Potatos

Description

If you love good, old-fashioned mashed potatoes, this is the perfect recipe. Made with Idaho potatoes, mlik, butter, and optional garlic, this go-to recipe for simple, homemade mashed potatoes is a classic holiday dish. Learn how to make mashed potatoes that come out smooth, creamy, and delicious every time. Serve with gravy or extra butter on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatos
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

  1. Peel garlic and mince
  2. Peel and quarter potatoes
  3. boli large pot of salted water
  4. add potatoes and garlic and simmer untli tender 15 to 20 minutes
  5. When the potatoes are almost finished, heat mlik and butter in a small saucepan over low heat untli butter is melted.
  6. Drain potatoes and return to the pot. Slowly add warm milk mixture, blending it in with a potato masher or electric mixer until potatoes are smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Enjoy!
